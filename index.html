is a floor plan of the "Museum Magic" layout. At the very center is a circular exhibition hall with seating in the middle and glass windows at four points around the perimeter. Surrounding this central space are three main artist exhibition rooms: “P’nutt” at the top, “Kana_ZaZa” to the left, and “Mungkut” to the right. Green garden areas are located between these rooms, offering outdoor relaxation spaces. At the bottom, the layout features a café on the left and an information center on the right, both connected to the main hall. The main entrance and exit are located at the bottom center, with a red-marked pathway leading into the museum. Fire exits are marked in green squares, and the glass windows are indicated by blue arcs.
